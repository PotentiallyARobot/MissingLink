MARKUP
How markup works

Draw colored regions on your image. Then reference them in your prompt by color — the model will target exactly those areas.

Red — the area you want to change
"Replace what's in the red box with a mountain"
Blue — what to preserve
"Keep everything in blue exactly the same"
Green — focus on or highlight
"Enhance the detail in the green area"
Yellow — a second reference
"Match the yellow area's color palette"

Your original image is never changed. Marks are layered on at Generate time and cleared after. You can mark each slot independently.
